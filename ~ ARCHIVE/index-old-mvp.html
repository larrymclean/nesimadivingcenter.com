Welcome to Nesima Diving Center

We are a PADI 5-Star Gold Palm Resort on the beachfront promenade of Dahab, Egypt — one of the Red Sea's most beloved dive destinations. Our adventure started in the early 1990s and diving has been our passion and lifestyle ever since.

Whether you're taking your very first breath underwater or leading your hundredth dive, our team of 15 multilingual PADI Instructors will make every dive unforgettable. We teach and guide in English, Arabic, German, French, and Polish.

🤿 PADI Certified 🏖️ Beachfront 🏠 Accommodation 🌍 5 Languages ✅ CDWS Accredited

Dahab & Beyond — Our Trips

Day trips and safari expeditions by boat, jeep, and camel.

🔵

Blue Hole & Bells

Dahab's most famous dive sites — the world-renowned Blue Hole and the dramatic Bells drop-off. By jeep.

⚓

SS Thistlegorm

The Red Sea's most iconic wreck — a WWII British cargo ship with motorbikes, trucks, and rifles still aboard.

🏜️

Ras Abu Galoum

Remote and pristine dive sites accessible by camel safari through the Sinai desert. A truly magical experience.

🦈

Ras Mohamed

Egypt's most famous national park — Shark Reef, Yolanda Reef, and walls draped in coral meeting schooling barracuda.

🌊

Gabr el Bent

A local favorite — dramatic underwater topography, vibrant coral, and prolific marine life north of Dahab.

🐠

Lighthouse & Canyon

Dahab's classic shore dives — accessible year-round, suitable for all levels, with incredible reef life.

Snorkeling Adventures

Dahab's shallow reefs, clear warm water, and calm conditions make it one of the world's best snorkeling destinations. Our guided trips are perfect for families, couples, and non-divers who want to experience the Red Sea.

  • ✓ Guided by experienced staff
