Dahab's Legendary Sites

Shore access from our beachfront location — no boat required for most dives.

🔵

Blue Hole

The world's most famous dive site — a vertical shaft descending 100m. The saddle at 6m is safe for all levels; the arch is for experienced divers.

6–100m Shore All levels*
Book This Dive
🏔️

The Canyon

A dramatic underwater canyon with swim-throughs, caverns, and dramatic rock formations. One of Dahab's most photogenic dives.

10–52m Shore Advanced
Book This Dive
🐠

Eel Garden

A shallow sandy slope carpeted with hundreds of garden eels. Perfect for beginners and macro photography. Gentle conditions year-round.

5–20m Shore Beginner
Book This Dive
🪸

Lighthouse Reef

Nesima's house reef — right in front of our center. Colourful hard corals, turtles, and vibrant fish life. Perfect first dive and night dive site.

3–25m House Reef All levels
Book This Dive

*Blue Hole saddle suitable for Open Water divers. Arch requires Advanced certification.
