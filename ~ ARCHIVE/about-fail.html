OUR STORY

DAHAB  •  RED SEA  •  SINCE THE 1990S

Three decades of passion, professionalism, and the perfect dive.

Born from a Love of the Sea

Nesima Diving Center was founded in the early 1990s by divers who fell so deeply in love with Dahab's underwater world that they never left. What began as a small beach operation grew into one of the Red Sea's most respected dive centers — built on one unshakeable belief: every person deserves to experience the ocean.

We have guided first-time breathers and seasoned technical divers alike. We have watched nervous beginners transform into confident open water divers over the course of a single week.

Three decades later, the feeling is still the same: every morning we look out at the Red Sea and know we're exactly where we're supposed to be.

Dahab Is Home

We don't just work in Dahab — we live here. Our team knows every dive site, every current pattern, every season. We know which sites are best on a north wind and which ones light up at sunset.

That local knowledge is the difference between a good dive and the dive of your life. It's why our guides take groups of 4, not 12. It's why we choose the site based on conditions, not convenience.

Dahab is not just a destination. For us, it's everything.
